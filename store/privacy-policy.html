Privacy Policy

Grittt · Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Grittt (“Grittt,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Grittt mobile application (the “App”). Grittt is a personal discipline and habit-tracking app that helps you build better habits and reduce unwanted ones. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, profile picture, and Google account identifier to create and secure your account.
  • App content you create. The habits you track, check-ins, streaks, counts, discipline scores, challenges you join, and any profile details you enter.
  • Microphone / audio (optional). If you use a feature that requires it, the App may access your microphone only while you actively use that feature. Audio is used solely to provide that feature and is not recorded in the background.
  • Technical data. Basic device and usage information needed to operate the service reliably (e.g., error logs).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the App and sync your data across sessions and devices.
  • To calculate streaks, discipline scores, and progress.
  • To generate personalized insights and motivational messages (see Third-Party Services below).
  • To maintain security and improve reliability.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted providers to operate the App:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication.
  • OpenAI — to generate AI-based insights and motivational nudges. When you use these features, relevant habit context (such as a habit name and streak length) is sent to the provider to generate a response. It is not used to identify you.
  • Cloud hosting (Amazon Web Services) — to host the backend and store your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers above, strictly to operate the App, or where required by law.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below. We will delete your data within a reasonable period unless retention is required by law.

6. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and stored on access-controlled servers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

7. Children

Grittt is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
